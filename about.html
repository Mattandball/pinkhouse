About Us

We are passionate about coffee and creating a cozy atmosphere for our customers.

Our journey began in a small coffee roastery, where we carefully selected the finest coffee beans from around the world. With dedication and love for the craft, we have grown into the Pink House Coffee Shop.

Our mission is to serve you the best coffee and pastries while providing a warm and welcoming environment where you can relax and enjoy your favorite brew.
